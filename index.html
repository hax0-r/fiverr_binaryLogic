Our Questions & Answers

FuelPay is a digital fuel payment platform designed for fuel stations and corporate fleets. It supports payments through NFC smart cards, mobile apps, POS systems, and QR-based solutions, ensuring secure and efficient transactions.

FuelPay supports prepaid cards, national subsidy discount cards, corporate fleet cards, secure QR-based vouchers, and mobile payments via NFC technology and QR codes.

The POS Management System allows fuel stations to remotely configure, update, and monitor POS devices, ensuring smooth operations across multiple locations with minimal downtime.

Binary Logic uses a scalable microservices architecture, advanced integrations with failover mechanisms, and a secure, mobile-ready platform to ensure reliable and safe transactions.

Binary Logic is Oman’s leading NFC card supplier, offering advanced technology, high uptime (99.99%), real-time fleet expense control, and comprehensive support for seamless operations.
