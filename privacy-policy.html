Technical Data: Such as IP address, device type, browser details, and visit duration automatically collected via analytics tools and cookies.
Cookies: Data collected through cookies to personalize user experience and monitor site performance.

How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:
Respond to inquiries and requests
Improve website content and functionality
Understand how users interact with our website

Our website uses cookies and similar technologies to:
Analyze visitor behavior
Customize content based on user preferences

Users can modify their cookie settings in their browser at any time.

Sharing of Information

We do not sell or rent your personal data. We may share limited information with:

Data Security

We implement technical and organizational safeguards to protect your data from unauthorized access, disclosure, or misuse.

User Rights
